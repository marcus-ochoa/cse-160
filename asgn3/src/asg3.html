Assignment 3: Blocky World

Please use a browser that supports "canvas"
ms: 0 fps: 0



=== MOVEMENT ===
WASD to move cardinally
SHIFT + W to move up
SHIFT + S to move down
Q to pan left
E to pan right
Alternatively:
HOLD LEFT CLICK + DRAG to pan

=== INTERACTION ===
F to add block in front of you
R to remove block in front of you


Grass and dirt textures from Kenney Assets: https://kenney.nl/assets/retro-textures-1
